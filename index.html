HOW TO PLAY

How to Play

Basic Controls

Use your mouse to pick up a tile and drop it on top of an adjacent tile.

Tiles can only be moved one space in four directions:

UP
DOWN
LEFT
RIGHT

Making an incorrect move will not result in a penalty, but the tiles will not swap colours.

A timer will start counting down once you click

When the timer reaches zero, the game will end.

Buttons

Begins a new game and resets the score and timer.

Pauses the current game and stops the timer.

Resumes a paused game and starts the timer.

Changes the game timer to 30 or 60 seconds.

Turns sound effects on / off.

Match Up

There are 6 different colours in the game area:

Your objective is to match these colours in rows of 3, 4, or 5.

Horizontal and vertical matches can be made.

Score Points

Create colour matches to score points.

Three points

Four points

Five points

The top three high scores will be displayed in the game interface.

Try to get the highest score you can!
